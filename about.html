About This Portal

A simple community system for residents and barangay services

© 2026 Community Portal. All rights reserved.

Our Purpose

The Community Portal was created to make barangay services more accessible and organized. Residents can request documents, view announcements, and stay updated with local news in one place.

Features

  • Online Resident Service Requests
  • Latest Community News
  • Important Announcements
  • Easy Navigation for Residents

Developer

This portal was designed and developed by SK Lee "Pogi" Alpas as a community-focused project to improve digital access to barangay services and information.
